{smr} {productnumber} is an open source infrastructure management solution, optimized and tailored specifically for the retail industry.
It is designed to help you reduce costs, optimize operations, and ensure compliance while increasing the flexibility, reliability, and uptime of your point of service devices.
{smr} provides a reliable, flexible, and open platform for managing point-of-service and point-of-sale terminals, kiosks, self-service systems, and reverse-vending systems.

This document provides an overview of {smr}, and guides you through initial installation and setup.

For more detailed information about managing your {smr} environment, or to find out where to get help, see <<retail_chap_next.adoc#retail.chap.next>>.
--


[[retail.sect.intro.what]]
== What is {smr}?

{smr} is designed for use in environments that include servers, workstations, point-of-service terminals, and other devices.
It allows administrators to configure the network topology of servers, build PXE-based operating system images, and manage the deployment and provisioning of target machines.
